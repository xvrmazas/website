How to Use These Tools

A Simple Three-Step Process

These tools work best when used together and in order. Here's how to get the most out of them.

1

Start with the Wheel of Life

3

Bring Both to Your Session

Your completed tools become the foundation of your first coaching conversation. No cold starts — we go straight to what matters.
